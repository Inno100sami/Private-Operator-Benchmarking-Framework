Commercial Performance

Revenue, billing efficiency, metering level, service complaints, disconnection rates, and accounts receivable — all benchmarked across operators.

Financial Analysis

Operating ratio, production costs, energy expenditure, and labour costs as a share of OPEX. Instantly see which operators are financially sustainable.

Operational Efficiency

Non-revenue water (NRW), pump functionality, continuity of supply, and network leakage frequency — the core operational health indicators.

Water coverage, average hours of supply per day, tariff levels, and complaint response times — measuring service delivery to end users.
